How to play this game

  • First Player will get first chance
  • Roll the dice unitl you want, your score will increase accordingly, but if you get 1 in any
    chance you current score will become zero and another player will get the chance
  • Hold will save your current score and chance will be given to another player
  • Player whose score reaches 50 first will win !!!
